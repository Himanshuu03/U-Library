DBMS Fun Facts

  • The term “Database Management Systems” or DBMS was coined in the late 1960s. Sometimes you may even refer a DBMS as a ‘database’.

  • The present day “Database Management Systems” have evolved from a class of programs known as “file management systems”.

  • The data base concept spread beyond the world of military contractors after it was popularized in the two symposia’s held in 1964 and 1965. These were organized by The System Development Corporation (SDC) of California in collaboration with military agencies.

  • In 1970s – these were promoted and sold to corporate entities as a technology that helped a company integrate all its data in a single place.
