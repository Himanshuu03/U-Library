App Devlopment Fun Facts

  • In 2019, just under $60 billion USD was spent driving app downloads. By 2022, that number is expected to be just under $120 billion USD.

  • Mobile shopping apps are the future. Over 50% of shoppers prefer shopping on mobile apps rather than mobile or desktop websites.

  • Most people only use a handful of apps. Despite there being over 5 million apps to choose from, most people use just 10 per day on average.

  • Facebook is winning the app race. Despite Google owning the most apps on the market, Facebook has the most popular apps both by daily download and daily active users.

  • There were over 200 billion app downloads last year. With roughly 218 billion downloads last year.
